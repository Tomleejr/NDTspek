I

Level I

Technician (supervised)

SNT-TC-1A · Level I Employer writes their own training requirements. Typical: 4–40 classroom hrs + 70–400 OJT hrs depending on method. Employer certifies you.
CP-189 · Level I Same levels as SNT-TC-1A but minimums are mandatory — employer cannot reduce them. ASNT Level III must oversee the program.
CP-105 · Level I Defines the exact topic outlines for Level I training in each method. Used by employers and training providers to build compliant courses.
ASNT 9712 · Level I Third-party certification — ASNT administers exams via Pearson VUE. Includes a hands-on practical. Cert is portable across employers.
II

Level II

Senior technician (independent)

SNT-TC-1A · Level II Additional training + 140–840 OJT hrs. Pass general, specific, and practical exams. Interpret results, make accept/reject calls, write reports.
CP-189 · Level II Stricter mandatory minimums. Required by nuclear, aerospace, and some ASME / AWS code work. Qualified instructor required for training.
CP-105 · Level II Specifies the syllabus for Level II in every method. A valid ASNT Level II cert can fulfil the general exam requirement under SNT-TC-1A.
ASNT 9712 · Level II Portable, ISO 9712-aligned. Hands-on practical on top of written exams. Required on many pipeline and international contracts.
III

Level III

Manager / expert

SNT-TC-1A · Level III Years of Level II experience + ASNT Basic exam + Method exam(s). Writes all procedures. Certifies others. Full program authority. Engineering degree can reduce experience requirement.
CP-189 · Level III Same as SNT-TC-1A Level III but must be an ASNT-certified Level III. Required to review and approve the employer's written practice.
CP-105 · Level III Provides the advanced topical outlines for Level III training in each NDT method. Used alongside SNT-TC-1A or CP-189 to build Level III qualification programs.
ASNT 9712 · Level III Highest portable certification. Full written + practical exams administered by ASNT. Fulfils the ASNT-certified Level III requirement under CP-189.
